About StudioBros

I'm Ethan, the creator of StudioBros! I created it because I have a passion for music production, and the only DAW that fits my budget and workflow is FL. It's easy to get started with, and a versatile tool for masters.

I use FL Studio 21.0 Producers' Edition. I sell my music on a service called Bandcamp for growing artists. I also make Black MIDIs for my YouTube channel, Musikid.
